Gamer's Guide

Welcome to a TIC TAC TOE Game by Ayorinde Adeshina

It is a Two Player Game. The first player automatically gets the letter "X", while the opponent is "O".

Each player is to attempt to have 3 of their letters aligned either horizontally, vertically or diagonally. The first person to achieve this wins.

Click on any of the boxes to begin.
